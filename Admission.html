Admissions



The Administration and Admissions office

As administration we nurture a child’s personal growth by providing a friendly and supportive environment. Our vision being that our commitment to serve has no bounds, we labour to facilitate quality education and civilization without reservation.

Every child leaves as a successful learner, a confident individual and a responsible citizen. We do keep parent's and child’s interest at heart.

Our Registration

Our office is open from 7:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. weekdays and 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. on Saturday throughout the year. You are welcome to register your child at any time during these days.

our admission office

The admissions office is the gate way to Glory To Glory School. New children get through the sieve of oral interviews, plus tests in Mathematics and English language.While our pass mark is 75%, the age and background of the child is taken into account on admission.

To confirm reservation of a place for the child after a successful interview, the Admission/Registration fee as per the obtaining school fees structure for the year should be fully paid.

The child is thereby officially admitted into the school. Parents and visitors are always welcome to be taken around for closer inspection of the school and its facilities.
